About Dr. Muhammad Usman Naeem

Dr. Muhammad Usman Naeem is a renowned gastroenterologist based in Lahore, known for his exceptional patient satisfaction rate. He holds the following qualifications: MBBS, FCPS.

With expertise in gastroenterology, Dr. Usman Naeem specializes in diagnosing, preventing, and treating diseases related to the digestive system, including the gastrointestinal tract, bile ducts, pancreas, gallbladder, liver, and more.

Over the years, he has served in various gastroenterology departments, successfully treating conditions such as indigestion, colitis, gastroesophageal reflux, and gallbladder issues.

Dr. Usman Naeem adopts both medical and surgical approaches to manage and treat digestive system conditions, providing personalized treatment plans for patients of all age groups.

His extensive professional practice has enhanced his skills across the broad field of gastroenterology, and he is adept at performing advanced gastroenterology procedures.

His dedication to advanced gastroenterology techniques makes him one of the leading certified gastroenterologists in Lahore.

Anti Reflux Measures

Dr. Muhammad Usman Naeem provides comprehensive guidelines for managing acid reflux. These measures include dietary recommendations, lifestyle modifications, and practical tips to alleviate symptoms and improve digestive health. Following these guidelines can help prevent discomfort and promote overall well-being.

Hepatic Encephalopathy: A Real Danger??

In this insightful article, Dr. Muhammad Usman Naeem delves into the complexities of hepatic encephalopathy, a serious condition associated with chronic liver disease. He explains the causes, symptoms, and treatment options available for managing this life-threatening illness. The article highlights the importance of early diagnosis and ongoing medical care for affected individuals.

Liver Involvement in Dengue Fever

Dr. Muhammad Usman Naeem explores the impact of dengue fever on liver health in this detailed publication. The article addresses how dengue fever affects liver function, the symptoms to watch for, and the necessary diagnostic tests. Dr. Naeem also discusses the management and treatment strategies to mitigate liver complications arising from dengue fever.
